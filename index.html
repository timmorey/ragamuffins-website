ABOUT

Hi there! I was born and raised in Portland, where I grew up in a family with great cooks and food lovers.

In 2006, I graduated with a Culinary Arts degree from SMCC and pursued a career in a local, Portland bakery. It was there I gained invaluable industry experience as well as made the best friends I could hope for.  I left the bakery for the corporate world, but I never stopped loving to bake-- seizing every birthday, potluck, and get together I could find. So eventually, over a bowl of Vietnamese noodles, I decided start up my own bakery.

My goal is to contribute something positive to people, and to me, nothing is more universally loved than food. I love making cheesecakes and desserts, and the unlimited versatility that they allow. I love taking classic flavors and combinations and turning them into over the top creations. My baked goods are made from scratch, including homemade caramel, icings, and shortbread for crusts. 

I hope you enjoy the goods, please know, I've enjoyed making them even more.
